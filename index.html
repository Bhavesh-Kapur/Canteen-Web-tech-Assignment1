Best tots on the Block

Tasmanian Produce,
US Flavour.

Established in Launceston in 2015, South Coast Canteen is based on contemporary fast food encountered on travels throughout the US. Specialising in Fried Chicken, sourcing as much of our produce locally as possible, we work closely with Northern Tasmanian businesses to bring a uniquely Tasmanian flavour.

Simple, fresh and delicious meals available for catering for groups of up to 150. We've worked in backyards, paddocks, carparks and on jetties; we are happy to work with you in creating a menu to your liking.
